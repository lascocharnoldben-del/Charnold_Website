Yongpyong Resort is one of South Korea’s largest and most popular ski resorts, located in Pyeongchang, Gangwon Province. It offers a variety of ski slopes and snowboarding courses suitable for beginners to advanced athletes. The resort also features modern accommodations, restaurants, and entertainment facilities for visitors. Yongpyong hosted events during the 2018 PyeongChang Winter Olympics, boosting its international reputation. It is a top destination for winter tourism, attracting both domestic and international travelers.
