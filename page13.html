Hallasan is the highest mountain in South Korea, located in the center of Jeju Island. It is a dormant volcano and a popular destination for hikers and nature lovers. The mountain is surrounded by Hallasan National Park, which features diverse flora, fauna, and scenic trails. Its summit offers breathtaking views of Jeju Island and the surrounding ocean. Hallasan is also an important part of Jeju’s natural and cultural heritage, attracting tourists year-round.
