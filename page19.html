Film and media tourism in South Korea involves visiting locations where popular movies, TV shows, and web series were filmed. Fans travel to sites featured in productions like Squid Game to relive scenes and take photos. These locations often include streets, buildings, parks, and cultural landmarks that appeared on screen. Film and media tourism allows visitors to experience Korean pop culture and entertainment firsthand. It has become an important part of South Korea’s tourism industry, attracting both domestic and international fans.
