Myeongdong

Myeongdong is a popular shopping district in Seoul, South Korea. It is well known for its fashion stores, cosmetic shops, and street food stalls. Many local and international brands can be found in this busy area. Tourists enjoy walking through the lively streets while shopping and trying Korean snacks. Because of its vibrant atmosphere, Myeongdong is one of the most visited places in Seoul.
