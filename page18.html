Medical tourism in South Korea involves traveling to the country for cosmetic surgery, advanced healthcare, and specialized medical treatments. South Korea is renowned for its high-quality hospitals, skilled doctors, and modern medical technology. Many international patients visit for procedures such as plastic surgery, dermatology, and health check-ups. This type of tourism combines healthcare with travel, allowing visitors to recover while exploring the country. South Korea has become a leading destination for medical tourism in Asia due to its reputation for safety and expertise.
