The Boryeong Mud Festival is a famous annual festival held in Boryeong, South Korea. It started to promote the health benefits of the mineral-rich mud from the area. Visitors can enjoy activities like mud wrestling, mud sliding, and mud baths. The festival attracts thousands of local and international tourists every year. It is known for its fun atmosphere and unique cultural experience.
