Seoul is the vibrant capital of South Korea, blending modern skyscrapers, historic palaces, and bustling shopping districts. It offers cultural attractions like Gyeongbokgung Palace, Bukchon Hanok Village, and traditional markets, making it a hub for both history and contemporary life.
Busan is a major port city in South Korea, famous for its beaches, seafood markets, and scenic coastal landscapes. Popular spots inc lude Haeundae Beach, Gamcheon Culture Village, and Jagalchi Fish Market, attracting tourists who enjoy both relaxation and cultural experiences.
