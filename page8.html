K-Drama tourism is the activity of visiting locations where popular Korean dramas were filmed. Many fans travel to these places to see the real settings of their favorite scenes. These locations often include parks, cafes, streets, and historical landmarks. Visiting these places allows tourists to experience Korean culture and entertainment in real life. Because of the global popularity of Korean dramas, K-Drama tourism has become an important part of South Korea’s tourism industry.
