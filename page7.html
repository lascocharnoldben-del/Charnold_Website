K‑pop is a popular music genre that originated in South Korea and has become a global cultural phenomenon. K-pop, short for Korean pop music, is a music genre from South Korea known for its catchy songs, synchronized dance routines, and visually striking performances. It blends various musical styles, including pop, hip-hop, R&B, and electronic music, appealing to a wide audience. K-pop idols undergo extensive training in singing, dancing, and stage presence before debuting in groups or as solo artists. The industry is also famous for its dedicated fan culture, with fans actively supporting their favorite groups worldwide. Today, K-pop has become a global phenomenon, influencing fashion, entertainment, and music trends across the world.
