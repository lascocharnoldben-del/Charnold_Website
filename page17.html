Technology tourism in South Korea involves visiting places that showcase the country’s advanced technological innovations and companies. Tourists can explore Samsung Experience Centers, LG Innovation Hubs, and tech museums to see cutting-edge electronics and gadgets. This type of tourism allows visitors to learn about Korea’s tech industry, try interactive exhibits, and experience futuristic technologies firsthand. It attracts both tech enthusiasts and casual visitors interested in modern innovations. Technology tourism has become an important part of South Korea’s cultural and educational tourism.
